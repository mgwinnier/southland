Highlighted Projects

Hillstead Subdivision

Hillstead Subdivision, located in Lavon, Texas, focuses on the detailed design, construction planning, and management of 298 residential lots, incorporating significant infrastructure development and providing comprehensive construction support services while facilitating future growth.

Located in Lavon, Texas, this project involves detailed design, construction planning, and project management for 298 residential lots. With a significant focus on infrastructure design, it includes approximately 15,000 linear feet of water mains, 12,000 linear feet of sanitary sewer, 8,000 linear feet of storm sewer, and 16,000 linear feet of roadways. The plan accommodates future development, aiming to achieve balanced earthwork and provides comprehensive construction support services.

WoodSpring and Stay Apt. Hotel

WoodSpring and Stay Apt. Hotel, located in Waco, Texas, involves detailed design, construction planning, and management for two hotels, along with coordination with city agencies, providing construction support services, and facilitating site-specific adjustments as needed.

Situated in the City of Waco, Texas, this project entails the detailed design, construction plan development, and project management for the WoodSpring and Stay Apt. Hotels. The scope of work includes analyzing existing conditions, preparing site measures, detailed grading, designing plans for water, sanitary sewer, storm sewer, and signage, paving plans, and other associated calculations.
