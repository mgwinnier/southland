About Southland Consulting Engineers

Southland Consulting Engineers is a leading civil design and consulting firm based in Dallas, Texas. We specialize in providing expert civil engineering services, including commercial site development, stormwater, water, wastewater, and construction management services for a diverse range of clients, such as residential, subdivisions, industrial sites, and municipal clients.

Our company is owned and operated by Jorge Gonzalez, PE, who has amassed over 20 years of experience in delivering professional services throughout Texas. Jorge has fostered numerous professional and agency relationships, enabling Southland Consulting Engineers to provide comprehensive planning, permitting, and construction administration services beyond the realm of engineering.
